After establishing a peer connection, each peer will query each other for any additional peers so they can 'learn' of other potential peers to connect to.

(A peer may already be connected to other nodes, for example)

If additional potential peers are learnt about, the signaling process between peers kicks in, and additional peer connections are established amongst all the peers it knows about.
